ABOUT VITS

VITS is the ICT society of St. Sylvester's College Kandy and is one of the most ambitious in Sri Lanka. Renowned for its exceptional online and physical projects and events, VITS has consistently demonstrated excellence by securing numerous victories and titles in the field of Information and Communication Technology. The society's achievements include innovative competitions, workshops, and community service initiatives that foster technological skills among students and contribute to the broader ICT community. Through a blend of creativity, dedication, and teamwork, VITS continues to set the standard for ICT societies across the nation, inspiring future generations of tech enthusiasts.
