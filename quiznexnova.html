Quiz Competition

Instructions:

  • Eligibility: Students in Grades 10 to 13.
  • 4 members are allowed per team (Including the Captain).
  • No changes are allowed after registration.
  • Requirements: Stable internet connection and compatible devices for online rounds.
  • Quiz Format: Multiple-choice. Details on questions and scoring will be shared in the WhatsApp group.
  • Dates and times will be informed via WhatsApp. Teams must be available for all rounds.
  • No external help or collaboration. Cheating leads to disqualification.
  • Top teams from online rounds move to the finals at Nexnova '24.
  • Judges decision will be final.
