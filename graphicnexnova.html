Graphic Designing

  • Theme - Networking and Telecommunication.
  • Categories:
    • Grade 6 - 9
    • Grade 10 - 13
    • Each school can submit up to 2 entries per category, totaling 4 submissions.

Competitions:

  1. Infographics on Network Technologies:
    • Create infographics explaining technologies like Wi-Fi, 5G, and fiber optics.
  2. Poster Design for Telecommunication Events:
    • Design posters promoting telecommunication events or highlighting the importance of networking.
  3. Illustrations of Future Technologies:
    • Illustrate futuristic networking and telecommunication concepts, such as smart cities and IoT.
  4. Awareness Posters:
    • Create posters raising awareness about issues like cybersecurity, digital divide, and data privacy.

Choose one of the above categories.

Rules:

  • Posters must relate to networking and telecommunication themes.
  • Submissions must be original creations of the participant.
  • Any use of copyrighted material must be properly credited or legally permissible under fair use guidelines.
  • Posters should be in 3:4 (vertical) or 1:1 (square) aspect ratio.
  • Acceptable file formats: JPEG, PNG, or PDF, with a minimum resolution of 300 dpi.
  • Posters should be clear, visually appealing, and effective in communication.
  • Each participant may submit only one poster (up to 4 posters per school).
  • Entries will be judged based on creativity, relevance to the theme, visual impact, and overall presentation.
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding.
